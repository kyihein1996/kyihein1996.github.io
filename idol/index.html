Description

Early life and education

Zeen was born on 25 August 1980 in Mandalay, Burma (now Myanmar), but grew up and studied in New York City, United States, and returned in Myanmar in 2009. He graduated with a degree in music at City College of New York and Film Scoring at institute of Audio Research.

Career

Zeen released his first solo album The Lost World in 2009. He made his action debut with leading role in the 2018 film Mudras Calling alongside actress Hla Yin Kyae, directed by his wife Christna Kyi, and which premiered in Myanmar cinmas on 16 March 2018 which was a huge commercial success, topping film ratingss and becoming the most hit film at that time.[3][4][5] In 2017, he starred in his second film Deception (Oo Pel Dan Myin) alongside Thet Mon Myint and Kaew Korravee. The film is directed by the same director of Mudras Calling. Christina Kyi, premiered in Myanmar cinmas on 19 January 2018 stayed in local theaters for a record seven weeks and was also screened in Singapore.[4]The two cilms was both a domestic hit, and led to increased reconition for Zenn Kyi.[6][7] He was nominated for the 2018 Myanmar Academy Award for Best Actor. Now & Ever (2019) earned 3.5 billion kyat at the box office in Myanmar, becoming one of the most successful domestic films of the decade.
